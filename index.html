Paul William Walker IV (September 12, 1973 - November 30, 2013) was an American action Movie Star. He became well known in 1999 after his role in the hit film Varsity Blues. However, he was perhaps best known for his role as Brain O'Conner in The Fast and the Furious film series. His other films include Eight Below, Into the Blue, She's All That and Takers.
